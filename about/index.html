About Us

Crafting Dream South Indian Vacations

At BMC Holidays, we’re passionate about showcasing the magic of South India. Established in 2015, we’ve grown into the No. 1 provider of South India tour packages, crafting unforgettable experiences for discerning travelers. Our commitment to your safety and satisfaction is paramount. We’ve been recognized as one of the safest tour experience providers in south India. But what truly sets us apart is our dedication to personalized support. We understand that every traveler has unique dreams and desires. Our team of experts works closely with you to design a South Indian itinerary that perfectly captures your interests, pace, and budget.

The Visionary Behind BMC Holidays

The driving force behind BMC Holidays is Manjunath, fondly known as VJ Manju in Karnataka. He’s a multi-faceted personality – a celebrated anchor on Kannada’s Udaya TV, a star cricketer who played for Kannada celebrity cricket leagues like Dr Raj Cup, Television Premier League Kannada, Legendary Cinema Cup, etc., and a talented movie actor, and a successful entrepreneur. Manju’s passion for travel and his deep-rooted connection to South India are the cornerstones of BMC Holidays. He brings a unique perspective to travel, ensuring our tours offer a blend of must-see landmarks and hidden gems, all experienced with the warmth and insider knowledge that only a local can provide.

Join us on an unforgettable journey through South India! Let BMC Holidays create a personalized itinerary that unveils the region’s rich tapestry of culture, heritage, and breathtaking landscapes.

Let the adventure begin!
