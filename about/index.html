BMCHolidays offers a variety of South India tours covering popular destinations across Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, and Kerala. Here are some highlights: Karnataka: Explore the bustling city of Bangalore, relax in the coffee plantations of Coorg, or visit the beautiful beaches of Gokarna. Tamil Nadu: Trek the scenic Nilgiris mountains, marvel at the historical temples of Madurai, or soak up the cool climate in hill stations like Ooty and Kodaikanal. Kerala: Cruise the serene backwaters of Alleppey, witness the wildlife in Thekkady, or discover the beaches and charming towns along the Kerala coast. This is just a taste of what BMCHolidays offers. With their tours, you can experience the rich culture, stunning landscapes, and warm hospitality of South India.

The Indian Rupee (INR) is the official currency. You can exchange your currency at airports or with authorized money changers. Many tourist destinations also accept major credit cards.

We use a comfortable mix of air-conditioned cars, coaches, local trains, and even houseboats depending on the itinerary.

South India boasts a delicious and diverse cuisine. We will include breakfast in most packages and suggest a variety of restaurants for lunch and dinner to suit your taste and budget. We can also cater to dietary restrictions with prior notice.

BMCHolidays uses a comfortable mix of vehicles to ensure a pleasant travel experience for you. Air-conditioned sedans or buses are used for most sightseeing and transfers within your tour itinerary. They seat anywhere from 4 to 50 people, making them suitable for groups of all sizes. The spacious interiors allow for comfortable luggage storage. For journeys to hill stations, BMCHolidays recommends MPVs (multi-purpose vehicles). These provide more ride height and comfort compared to sedans, making them ideal for navigating hilly terrains.
